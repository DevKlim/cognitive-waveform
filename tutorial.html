How to Use the Visualizer

Now that you understand the visualizations, here's how to use the full Cognify visualizer interface to explore your data.

When using the full visualizer:

1

Select Subject & Metric

Use the dropdown menus at the top to choose which subject and data metric (heart rate, temperature, etc.) to visualize.

2

Explore the Chart

Click and drag on the chart to see data values at different times. Hover to see exact values in the tooltip.

3

Control Playback

Use the play button to animate the data over time. Skip buttons let you navigate quickly, and the slider controls playback speed.

4

Customize Sound

Change the sound type, adjust volume, or even upload your own audio to hear how it changes with the data values.

Pro Tips:

  • Press and hold the skip buttons to move quickly through the data
  • Press the sidebar button (top left) to switch between datasets
  • Try comparing different subjects by switching between them
  • For a deeper experience, upload your own music and hear how it morphs with the data
